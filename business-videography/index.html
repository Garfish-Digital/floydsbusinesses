Behind the Lens

I’m Floyd, an independent videographer in Indianapolis. I work with local artists, musicians, small businesses, and people just like you to create visuals that feel as real as the places they come from.

My style is raw but polished — urban documentary meets cinematic storytelling. Whether I’m shooting in a graffiti-covered alley or a packed venue, I aim to make every frame feel alive.

I shoot primarily with Sony mirrorless gear and a lightweight gimbal rig, making me agile for fast-paced shoots without compromising quality.
